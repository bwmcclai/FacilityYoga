About the Studio

Every body
moves.

Facility Yoga & Dance is built on a simple belief: movement is for everyone. Every age, every shape, every size. Whether you're a young athlete sharpening your agility, a child discovering the joy of dance, or someone stepping onto the mat for the very first time — this is your space.

Our intimate studio offers individual and small group yoga, dance, and movement classes designed to meet you exactly where you are. We use movement to improve agility, build confidence, and create a welcoming space where every body belongs.
